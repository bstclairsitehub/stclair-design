About Vanguard

Founded in 2006 by William Vanguard, a former Sotheby's International Realty Director, Vanguard Estates has become the premier luxury real estate brokerage serving the world's most discerning clientele. With $2.8 billion in completed transactions and a portfolio of 340+ premium properties, we've established ourselves as the gold standard in luxury real estate.

Our philosophy is simple: we don't sell homes—we unlock lives. Every property we represent is carefully curated. Every client relationship is built on trust, transparency, and results.
