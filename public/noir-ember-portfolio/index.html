01 / OUR STORY

Chef Marcus Bellamy

Trained under René Redzepi at Noma, Copenhagen, where he spent two years understanding minimalism and Nordic principles. Marcus then moved to The Fat Duck with Heston Blumenthal for three intensive years, mastering molecular gastronomy and the science of flavor. Returning to Chicago in 2018, he opened Noir & Ember on West Randolph Street—a restaurant where fire becomes philosophy. Michelin recognition followed within 18 months, validating his radical approach to the open flame as the soul of modern cooking.
