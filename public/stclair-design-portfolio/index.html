WHO WE ARE

St Clair Design was founded in 2019 by Berlin St Clair in Los Angeles, born from a singular frustration: the lack of bold, intentional design in the digital space. What started as a solo mission has grown into a tight-knit team of designers, developers, and strategists who share an obsession with crafting work that matters.

We work with visionary founders, forward-thinking brands, and ambitious companies who understand that design is not decoration—it's strategy. Our approach blends strategic thinking with meticulous craftsmanship, resulting in digital products and brands that don't just look good, they perform exceptionally.

Over six years, we've had the privilege of partnering with over 120 brands across fashion, technology, hospitality, and real estate. Our work has been featured in Awwwards, CSS Design Awards, and FWA. But our real metric of success is our clients: 98% retention rate and $4M+ in revenue generated for our partners.
