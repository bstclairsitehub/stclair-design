WHO WE ARE

Apex Capital Partners was founded in 2006 by veteran quantitative researchers from MIT and Goldman Sachs, driven by a singular vision: to apply rigorous mathematical frameworks to capital markets with institutional discipline. Over nearly two decades, we've built a reputation for delivering consistent, superior returns through systematic multi-factor strategies that adapt seamlessly to evolving market regimes.

Our approach combines proprietary machine learning models with traditional factor analysis, spanning global equities, macro overlays, and volatility arbitrage. With $2.8 billion in assets under management and a minimum investment of $50 million, we serve family offices, foundations, and institutional allocators seeking nuanced exposure to liquid markets with exceptional risk management. Our team remains deeply invested in the success of our partners—the founder principals maintain significant personal capital alongside client assets.
